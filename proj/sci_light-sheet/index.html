Snapshot Compressive Volumetric Light-sheet Microscopy

Xukang Wang, Yang Liu, Xiaofei Han, Jinli Suo, Qionghai Dai
April 2019
project pdf slides doi bibtex

Abstract

We proposed a snapshot compressive volumetric light-sheet microscopy method for high-speed three-dimensional imaging of zebrafish and cleared mouse brain.
