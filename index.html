Biography

Yang Liu is a master student at the Broadband Networks and Digital Media Lab, Department of Automation, Tsinghua University, advised by Qionghai Dai. He emphasizes on computational imaging combined with compressive sensing and machine learning techniques. His current interests include high-throughput imaging, imaging through scattering media and single-pixel imaging. [CV]
